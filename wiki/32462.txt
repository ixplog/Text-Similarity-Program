The Virgin Islands are the western island group of the Leeward Islands  which are the northern part of the Lesser Antilles  and form the border between the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean  Politically  the eastern islands form the British Virgin Islands and the western ones form the Virgin Islands of the United States  The British Virgin Islands is an overseas territory of the United Kingdom comprising Tortola  Virgin Gorda  Jost Van Dyke and Anegada   The U S  Virgin Islands is one of five inhabited insular areas of the United States  along with American Samoa  Guam  Northern Mariana Islands  and Puerto Rico  The territory comprises St  Croix  St  John  St  Thomas and Water Island   The Virgin Passage separates the U S  Virgin Islands from the so called Spanish Virgin Islands of Vieques and Culebra  which are part of Puerto Rico  The United States dollar is the official currency on both the British and U S  Virgin Islands as well as the Spanish Puerto Rican Virgin
