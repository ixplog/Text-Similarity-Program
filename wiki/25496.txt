The Republic of the Congo is located in the western part of Central Africa  Situated on the Equator  it is bordered by the Angola exclave of Cabinda to the south  201 km   the Democratic Republic of the Congo to the south and east  2 410 km   the Central African Republic  467 km  and Cameroon  523 km  to the north and Gabon to the west  1 903 km   Congo has a 169 km long Atlantic coast with several important ports  The Republic of the Congo covers an area of 342 000 km²  of which 341 500 km² is land while 500 km² is water  Congo claims of territorial sea  The capital of the Republic of the Congo is Brazzaville  located on the Congo river immediately across from Kinshasa  capital of the Democratic Republic of the Congo  With a metropolitan population of approximately 1 5 million  Brazzaville is by far the largest city in the Republic  having almost twice the population of Pointe Noire  663 400 as of the 2005 census   the country s second largest city  About 70% of the
