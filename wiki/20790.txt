Mary  mother of John Mark is mentioned in the Acts of the Apostles 12 12  which says that  after his escape from prison  Peter went to her house   When he realized this  he went to the house of Mary  the mother of John whose other name was Mark  where many were gathered together and were praying  This seems to be the only mention of her in the Bible   The question whether her son John Mark can be identified with others called Mark or John in the New Testament is discussed in the article about him  Greek scholars reject his identification with Mark the Evangelist and have a separate feast for John Mark in their synaxarion  Catholic scholars are divided on this issue   1  References  Easton s Bible Dictionary  1897
